Track your personal expenses on the go with this free easy-to-use app. This app would help you to make a budget and track your expenses in a simple way. Use ExpenseTracker to record the following types of expenses:
  • Your weekly household budget
  • Costs of various unexpected expenditure
  • Holiday or travel expenses
  • Coffees, lunches and any other cash expenses that you find hard to record
  • Separate 'needs' and 'wants' to identify opportunities to save
  • View your expense history
  • View items on which you spend most usually
  • Maintain photos of bills,various expenses and keep it for your records,all in one place
  • View expenses in a particular period
